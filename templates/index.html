Welcome to Structures Energy Efficiency predictor

This project uses machine learning to predict Structures Energy Efficiency based on various factors. Enter the details on the prediction page, and the system will provide an estimate of the corresponding Energy Efficiency values.

Factors considered include:

  • 1-Relative Compactness (ratio):
    • Description: Represents the ratio of the compactness of the building.
    • Input Range: 0.0 to 1.0
  • 2-Overall Height (m):
    • Description: Indicates the overall height of the building.
    • Input Range: 0.0 to 10.0 meters
  • 3-Orientation (Degrees):
    • Description: Denotes the orientation of the building in degrees.
    • Input Range: 0.0 to 10.0 degrees
  • 4-Glazing Area (mÂ²):
    • Description: Refers to the glazed area of the building.
    • Input Range: 0.0 to 10.0 square meters
  • 5-Glazing_Orientation:
    • Description: Represents the orientation of glazing.
    • Input Range: 0.0 to 10.0
  • 6-Aspect Ratio:
    • Description: Denotes the aspect ratio of the building.
    • Input Range: 0.0 to 500
  • 7-Total Area:
    • Description: Represents the total area of the building.
    • Input Range: 0.0 to 2000
